More Cool Facts

Snakes have been around for a while, here's what they're known for:

  • There are around 3000 different species of snake
  • Have a unique anatomy which allows them to swallow and digest large prey
  • Shed their skin a number of times a year in a process that usually lasts a few days
  • Some species use venom to hunt and kill prey
  • Smell with their tongue
